Project Overview

This simulator provides an interactive way to understand how different disk scheduling algorithms work. You can generate custom or random workloads and see the head movement and metrics in real time.

  • Visual simulation of disk head movement on a cylinder axis.
  • Supports multiple algorithms: FCFS, SSTF, SCAN, and C-SCAN.
  • Shows total seek time, average seek time, and throughput.
  • Highlights the best-performing algorithm for each workload.
  • Exportable metrics for reports (CSV and PDF support).
